❤ Internote

Internote is a rich text editor designed for distraction-free content creation ✏📜.

Internote supports a wide variety of different formatting options including bold, italic and underline including combinations of the three as well as lists, code snippets, headings and quotes. There are also other useful features such as:

  • Integrated code editor
  • In-built Oxford dictionary
  • Text-to-speech at the press of a button
  • Focus mode for distraction-free writing
  • Multiple colour and font themes
  • Emojis 🚀 and #hashtags

Internote automatically saves your notes in the cloud and is completely free.
